Changed analysis behavior
- main.py: analyzes the full completed viewing list and tracks rated entries separately.
- analyzer/data.py: watched recurrence includes unrated shows; rating metrics use rated shows only.
- analyzer/profile.py: preserves rating-first behavior for sufficiently rated lists and uses full-history recurrence for sparse lists.
- analyzer/recommendations.py: adds a viewing-history recommendation path when rating coverage is sparse.
- analyzer/report.py: separates Watched and Rated counts and handles sparse or absent ratings throughout.
- analyzer/share.py and analyzer/share_pillow.py: image metrics now distinguish watched and rated anime.
- analyzer/models.py: group statistics include rated_count and allow missing rating metrics.
